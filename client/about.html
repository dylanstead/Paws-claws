About Us

We are two developers with a deep love for animals. Our passion for technology and our commitment to animal welfare led us to create Paws and Claws. Our mission is to help animals find loving homes and offer them a chance at a happy life.

At Paws and Claws, we believe in matching each dog with the right family and the perfect home. We understand that adopting a pet is a significant decision, and we're here to support you every step of the way.

Our team is dedicated to ensuring the well-being of every dog in our care. We provide medical attention, socialization, and lots of love while they wait for their forever homes.
